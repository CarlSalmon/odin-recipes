Power Porridge

Description

Wake up in the morning to this fresh, fruity and protein packed porridge!

These overnight oats are very quick and easy to grab on the go when your in a rush to head out the door. Perfect for a pre-workout with all the protein you'll need in the morning.

Ingredients (serves 1)

  • 100g Steel Cut Oats
  • 1 Handful of Blueberries
  • 1 tbsp Chia Seeds
  • 1 scoop Protein Powder
  • 1 cup Almond Milk
  • 1 tbsp Honey
